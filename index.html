About

Anokhi is distinguished by its prints and sense of designs, range of colours and product quality. For over 50 years, Anokhi’s ideals have been those of conservation and development, through the input of design, marketing and project funding. The Company is well known as an alternative role model for good business practices, and the ongoing revival of traditional textile skills.
